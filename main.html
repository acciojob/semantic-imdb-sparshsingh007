Summary

Chris Gardner, played by Will Smith, is a struggling salesman trying to support his family. Due to financial problems, his wife leaves, and Chris becomes homeless while raising his young son. Despite hardships, he joins an unpaid internship at a brokerage firm and works tirelessly for a better future. His dedication and love for his son finally lead him to success.

Actors

Will Smith as Chris Gardner, Jaden Smith as Christopher Gardner Jr., Thandiwe Newton as Linda Gardner.
